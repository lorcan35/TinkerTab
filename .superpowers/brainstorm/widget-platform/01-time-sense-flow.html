TIME SENSE · THE REFERENCE SKILL

The orb feels time with you.

A skill for ADHD brains. No numbers on screen (until the end). The orb's color + breathing rate is the entire interface — calm at the start, alive mid-flow, rose in the last minute. A short narrative line says what's happening, not what's counting. Dragon does the reasoning; Tab5 renders it.

This is the canonical v1 skill — everything the widget platform has to support fits inside it.

◦ The five states

One skill, five faces.

Each state is a widget_live update — same slots, different tone, progress, and narrative. On Tab5 the orb + copy + halo all shift in sync. Dragon decides the tone based on time remaining, not Tab5 — the skill is still just emitting state.

◦ Multi-timer

One orb. Many timers.

You can't show 5 orbs at once — but you can show one and know the rest are running. A small counter pill appears bottom-left; tap it to cycle, long-press to reveal the stack. Critical events auto-promote themselves to the orb regardless of what's in front. The brain picks the winner.

◦ Protocol

One skill, four messages.
